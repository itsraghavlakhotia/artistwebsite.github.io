About the artist

Namaste!

I'm Priyasha Rathi a creative passionate about beautifying spaces and finding joy in everyday moments. I believe in the power that colours have the ability to light our soul on fire, to awake our senses, to brighten up lives. As far as I could remember my love for art was not something I deveoped at a later stage, it was always present. I felt an unexplainable pull towards all things art from a young age.I painted a lot from my entire childhood, dipped in colours, pages on after the others fully immersed in those moments. I could not find any meditation purer than what art offered.So now, I'm beginning to give deeper in the whole process of creativity; as creativity is nothing but mind set free.
